The Renaissance Journey

From concert halls to jazz clubs, from recording studios to classrooms, from business leadership to code repositories - my journey spans the creative, academic, and entrepreneurial worlds.

With 25 years of comprehensive musical education spanning Classical, Jazz, and Music Technology, plus Practical Pedagogical Education, I've spent 6 years shaping minds in both public and private education while simultaneously building and leading my own music school for 5 years.

Currently pursuing my Master's in Communication, Design & Learning, I'm exploring how technology, creativity, and education intersect in our digital age. My recent ventures into finance and coding add yet another dimension to this interdisciplinary adventure.

When I'm not teaching, performing, or coding, you'll find me behind a camera lens or paintbrush, capturing and creating beauty in all its forms.
